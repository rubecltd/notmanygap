Child Neurology & Epilepsy

Child neurology and epilepsy is a specialized field of medicine that focuses on the diagnosis, treatment, and management of neurological disorders and epilepsy in children. Child neurologists are trained to evaluate and care for children with a wide range of neurological conditions.

Cerebral Palasy

Cerebral palsy is a neurological disorder that affects movement, muscle tone, and coordination. It is caused by damage or abnormalities in the developing brain, often occurring before or during birth. Symptoms vary in severity and can include difficulties with walking, fine motor skills, speech, and overall muscle control.

Autism

Autism is a developmental disorder characterized by difficulties in social interaction, communication, and repetitive behaviors. It is a spectrum disorder, meaning it affects individuals differently and to varying degrees. Autism is a developmental disorder characterized by difficulties in social interaction behaviors.

Early Intervention

Early intervention refers to the timely and targeted support provided to individuals, typically children, who are at risk of or experiencing developmental delays, disabilities, or other challenges. It aims to address these issues early on to maximize potential and promote positive outcomes in various areas of development.

Speech & Audiology

Child speech and audiology is a specialized field that focuses on the assessment and treatment of communication disorders in children, particularly related to speech and hearing.
And other related conditions to support a child's development and improve their communication skills.

Child Occupational Therapy

Child occupational therapy is a specialized form of therapy that focuses on helping children develop and improve the skills they need to participate in daily activities and reach their full potential. It addresses areas such as fine motor skills, sensory processing, And cognitive abilities through engaging, play-based interventions.

    UTAGE Child Care caters to children from infants to school-age. We have specialized programs tailored to meet the developmental needs of each age group.

    UTAGE Child Care operates from Monday to Saturday, from 10:00 AM to 6:00 PM. We understand the needs of working parents and strive to provide flexible options for drop-off and pick-up.
